# Problem Gambling Help
Page: https://coastalhorizons.org/services/problem-gambling-help/

- Coastal Horizons Center provides care for individuals and families affected by problem gambling.
- Services begin with a comprehensive clinical assessment to determine each person's needs, risk factors, and strengths.
- A trained problem gambling counselor works with you to develop a person-centered treatment plan that sets clear goals and outlines the clinical services and supports best suited to your situation.
- Contact Coastal Horizons Center to get started or to learn more about availability for in-person or telehealth appointments: https://coastalhorizons.org/contact-us/email-form/
- Download a Flyer: https://coastalhorizons.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/04/Problem-Gambling-flyer.pdf

## What We Offer
- Thorough assessment: screening and diagnostic evaluation to establish clinical need and identify co-occurring concerns.
- Individualized treatment plan: collaboratively created goals, measurable objectives, and a timeline for progress.
- Skilled counseling: evidence-based individual and family interventions delivered by counselors specially trained in problem gambling.
- Flexible delivery: services available in person or via secure telehealth.
- Care coordination and referrals: assistance connecting with financial counseling, legal resources, peer support, and other community services as needed.
