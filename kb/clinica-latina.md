## English
- We offer Behavioral and Mental Health Services to Individuals, Families and Couples.
- Clinica Latina provides treatment services in Spanish, and in a culturally comfortable setting for Latinos and their families in New Hanover, Brunswick, and Pender County.
- Payment Methods: Clinica Latina accepts cash, credit card, Medicaid, and 3rd party health insurance.
- Location: Our office is located at 609 Shipyard Blvd Suite 106 Wilmington, NC 28412
- Contact us at 910-769-1201 for more information or to schedule an appointment today!
- Download a Flyer: https://coastalhorizons.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/English-Flyer.2019.pdf
