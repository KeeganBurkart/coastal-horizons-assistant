# About Coastal Horizons Center

Coastal Horizons Center, Inc. is a nationally accredited (CARF), non-profit organization promoting healthier lives, stronger families, and safer communities. It provides evidence-based substance use, mental health, crisis intervention, justice, and wellness services in more than half of North Carolina's 100 counties. Coastal Horizons is a NCDHHS-certified Critical Access Behavioral Health Agency and a Trillium Health Resources Valued Provider.
