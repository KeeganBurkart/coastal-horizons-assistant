Notes:

- Crisis Intervention = Rape Crisis Center and/or Open House youth shelter coverage.
- Program-specific footprints: Intensive In-Home in 7 counties, Child First in 21 counties, HOMEBUILDERS® in 42 counties, Justice Services in 53 counties.
