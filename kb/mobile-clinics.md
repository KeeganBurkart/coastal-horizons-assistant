# Mobile Clinics

- Phone: 910-599-5595
- Coastal Horizons Mobile Clinics serve Brunswick, Columbus, and Bladen counties.
- Schedule: Monday through Friday, 9:30 am to 4 pm.
- Please call before coming onsite to the unit in order to ensure mobile clinic is physically available for delivery of services.

## Services Offered
- Assessments for Substance Use and Mental Health
- Psychiatric Medication Management
- Individual Therapy
- Peer Support Services
- Medication Assisted Treatment
- Primary Care Services (in person and telehealth)
