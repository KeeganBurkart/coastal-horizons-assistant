# Personnel Policies (for Coastal Horizons staff)

Coastal Horizons' Personnel Policies document (updated January 2026) is
available as a PDF (linked from the documents section toward the bottom of
https://coastalhorizons.org):
https://coastalhorizons.org/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/Personnel-Policies-2026.pdf

If a staff member asks about an HR policy, point them to the PDF and the
relevant section below. Do NOT paraphrase specific policy provisions (leave
amounts, discipline steps, etc.) — the authoritative text is the PDF, and for
questions about how a policy applies, staff should contact Human Resources
(Travis McCracken, VP of Human Resources) via the main office at 910-343-0145.

## Section guide
1. Administration — mission, values, code of ethics, governance
2. Recruitment, Hiring and Employment — EEO policy, types of employment, hiring of former clients
3. Employee Qualifications — job descriptions & classifications
4. Positions — job descriptions, new employee orientation, transfers and promotion
5. Credentialing and Privileging
6. Probationary Employment Period
7. Employee Responsibilities — office hours, remote/hybrid work, dress code, hazardous weather
8. Office Guidelines — equipment use, solicitation, tobacco-free campus, animals in buildings
9. Safety — fire and disaster plan, active shooter, injuries, incident reports, TB policy
10. Employee Benefits — health/dental, life insurance, retirement, disability, EAP, vacation, sick leave, holidays, FMLA, jury duty, bereavement, leaves of absence
11. Training and Career Development
12. Compensation — overtime, exempt/non-exempt
13. Payroll/Accounting/Travel — payroll, reimbursement, travel, client transportation, distracted driving
14. Supervision/Disciplinary/Termination — evaluations, progressive discipline, termination, grievance policy
15. Personnel Guidelines — PII policy, email/social media policies, staff-client relationships, conflicts of interest, confidential information
16. Workplace Policies — sexual harassment and other forms of harassment
