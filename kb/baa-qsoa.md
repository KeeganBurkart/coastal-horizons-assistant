# Business Associate / Qualified Service Organization Agreements (BAA/QSOA)

Coastal Horizons Center, Inc. is a HIPAA covered entity and operates federally
assisted Part 2 programs (42 CFR Part 2, substance use confidentiality rules).
Vendors, contractors, and partner organizations that handle protected health
information (PHI) on Coastal Horizons' behalf must sign a combined Business
Associate Agreement and Qualified Service Organization Agreement (BAA/QSOA).

Coastal Horizons has a master BAA/QSOA template (with Coastal Horizons as the
Covered Entity) covering HIPAA/HITECH business associate obligations plus
42 CFR Part 2 requirements for substance use treatment records.

To request or execute a BAA/QSOA, contact the main office at 910-343-0145.
Corporate compliance is overseen by Eric Luttmer, Vice President of Medical
Services and Corporate Compliance.
