# How to Make a Referral or Become a New Client

## Substance Use & Mental Health — New Patient & Client Inquiries (online form)
Many substance use programs accept electronic inquiries/referrals:
https://coastalhorizons.org/services/substance-use-mental-health/new-patient-inquiries-and-referrals/

The form asks for: your name/email/phone, relationship to referral (Self, Guardian, Family, Community Partner, Other), client name and date of birth, county, and desired program. If a desired program isn't listed, call or walk in.

Counties and programs available on the online form:
- Brunswick: IOP, Opioid Treatment, Child First, Intensive In-Home, Family Centered Treatment
- New Hanover: IOP, Opioid Treatment, Child First, Incredible Years, Intensive In-Home, Family Centered Treatment
- Pender: IOP, Opioid Treatment, Incredible Years, Child First, Intensive In-Home, Family Centered Treatment
- Bladen, Columbus, Onslow: Child First, Family Centered Treatment, Intensive In-Home

Program guide (when choosing on the form):
- IOP (Intensive Outpatient Program): 12-week program of group, individual, and family therapy plus case management
- OTP (Opioid Treatment Program): medication-assisted treatment for opioid addiction (methadone/buprenorphine)
- Incredible Years: parent education and support program
- Child First: infant/young-child mental health treatment & prevention, trauma-focused
- Intensive In-Home: for children/adolescents unstable in home and community due to serious emotional, behavioral, or substance use disorders
- Family Centered Treatment: evidence-based home-based family therapy and trauma treatment
