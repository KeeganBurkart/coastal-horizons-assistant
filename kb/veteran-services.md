# Veteran Services
Page: https://coastalhorizons.org/services/veteran-services/
Referrals page: https://coastalhorizons.org/services/substance-use-mental-health/veteran-services-inquiries-and-referrals/

- Coastal Horizons is partnering with Save a Vet Now (SAVN) to assist Veterans with access to and receipt of outpatient treatment services.
- Coastal Horizons is collaborating with SAVN through the development of our new Veterans Outreach Program.
- Save a Vet Now Inc.: www.saveavetnow.org
- The SAVN Veterans Outreach Program uses a special account to cover any co-pay or self-pay costs that are not covered by State, Medicaid, Medicare or other 3rd party insurance funding for treatment services at Brunswick, New Hanover or Pender outpatient locations.
- Veterans need to show "Proof of Veteran Status" to Front Desk and/or Patient Access staff.
- "Proof of Veteran Status" includes one of the following certificates: DD-214, DD-215, WD AGO 53/55, NAVMC 78-PD, or NAVPERS.
- Veterans can also demonstrate proof of service by showing the veteran designation on their driver's license.
- If you are, or know of a Veteran requiring services, complete the referrals form to be connected with someone in your/their selected County.
