## Open House Youth Shelter
- Open House Youth Shelter is committed to improving the safety and well-being of displaced abused, neglected and vulnerable youth.
- Children and adolescents ages 6-18 who are in need of a safe place to stay can receive shelter in the 24-hour, 9-bed facility.
- Services include food, clothing, shelter, counseling services, vocational/educational opportunities, life skill building, and recreational activities.
- All confidential services are provided at no cost to youth and their families.
- Services continue for a period of three months after discharge.
- Referrals are accepted 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
- Referrals are accepted from local agencies such as The Department of Social Services, law enforcement, Juvenile Justice, school personnel, and other youth serving organizations and professionals.
- Self-referrals and those from parents/legal guardians are also accepted.
- Open House is licensed biennially through the North Carolina Division of Social Services for temporary Emergency Foster Care placements as well as for long-term Residential/Foster Care services.

## Transitional and Maternity Housing Program
- Contact: tbellamy@coastalhorizons.org, gcarbone@coastalhorizons.org, or esjames@coastalhorizons.org.
- Voluntary program that supports youth and young adults aged 16-21 years old.
- Serves youth and young adults who are pregnant and /or parenting as well as their dependent children, who are homeless or at immediate risk of becoming homeless during their transition from adolescence into adulthood.

### Services Offered
- Support in attaining safe, stable housing
- Monthly rental assistance
- Individualized transitional living plans
- Weekly case management
- Employment and education assistance
- Monthly Life Skills programming
- Social engagement opportunities
- General, dental, and mental health service coordination
- Welfare services coordination as needed
- Transportation assistance as needed
- 24/7 support and crisis response
- Parenting skills
- Child Development
- Family budgeting/money management

## Street Outreach Program
- Contact: mtellup@coastalhorizons.org
- Voluntary program serving young adults aged 16-21 years old.
- Works with homeless, runaway and street youth to help them find stable housing and services.
- Goal: prevent the sexual exploitation and abuse of youth on the streets.

### Services Offered
- Street-based education and outreach
- Access to emergency shelter
- Survival aid
- Referrals to treatment and counseling
- Crisis intervention
- Follow-up support
